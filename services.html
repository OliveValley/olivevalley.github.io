Our work is rooted in research that informs actionable policy.

We support clients with:

3. Grant Writing & Fund Development

Access to funding is a common barrier for impactful projects.

We help clients:

  • Identify and align with appropriate funders
  • Develop competitive funding proposals and applications
  • Build logic frameworks, impact metrics, and supporting documentation

Projects fail not just from poor design but also from behavioral or systemic blind spots.

Our unique service includes:

  • Human-system integration in project delivery
  • Recommendations for performance optimization and team resilience

5. Capacity Building & Technical Training

We offer workshops and hands-on support to build internal capacity for sustainability implementation.

Topics include:

  • Climate risk integration
  • Monitoring & evaluation (M&E) for development projects
