❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What kind of organizations do you work with?

We work with a wide range of clients — from municipalities, NGOs, and academic institutions to private developers and grassroots initiatives.

Whether you're launching a new idea or scaling an existing program, we tailor our support to your needs and capacity.

2. Do you only operate in South Africa?

Our core base is in South Africa, but we collaborate across Africa and BRICS countries.

We’re especially interested in projects that aim to localize global sustainability goals.

3. What is green infrastructure and why is it important?

Green infrastructure refers to systems that use natural processes to deliver environmental and social benefits — such as reducing heat, improving air quality, managing stormwater, and enhancing urban resilience.

It’s key to adapting cities to climate change in a cost-effective and inclusive way.

4. Can you help us access funding for our project?

Yes.

Our grant writing and advisory service is designed to connect under-resourced organizations with local and international funding opportunities.

We develop full proposals, impact frameworks, and supporting documentation.

5. Do you offer training or workshops?

Absolutely.

We deliver both online and in-person training sessions on topics like sustainable project design, climate risk integration, and proposal development.

Custom workshops are also available.

6. How do you price your services?

We offer flexible pricing models including flat rates, success-based fees (for grant writing), retainers for ongoing advisory, and per-session pricing for training.

Let’s talk about what works for your budget and project scale.
